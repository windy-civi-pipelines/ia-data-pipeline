Section 1B.3, Code 2025, is amended to read as 1 follows: 2 1B.3 Flags on public buildings. 3 1. It shall be the duty of any A board of public officers 4 charged with providing supplies for a public building in the 5 state to shall provide a suitable state flag , and it shall be 6 the duty of the custodian of that public building to shall 7 raise the flags of the United States of America and the state 8 of Iowa , upon each secular day when weather conditions are 9 favorable. 10 2. The custodian of a public building is authorized to raise 11 the national league of families POW/MIA flag upon each secular 12 day when weather conditions are favorable. If the 27 national league of families POW/MIA flag is flown by a public 28 building, it must be flown second in order of precedence, 29 directly underneath, or adjacent to, the flag of the United 30 States of America.
