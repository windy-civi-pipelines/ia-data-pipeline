4 2025, is amended to read as follows:
5 d. (1) Supplementary weighting pursuant to this subsection
6 shall be available to a school district during the period
7 commencing with the budget year beginning July 1, 2014, through
8 the budget year beginning July 1, 2034. The maximum amount
9 of additional weighting for which a school district shall be
10 eligible in a budget year is twenty-one additional pupils;
11 provided, however, that for budget years beginning on or after
12 July 1, 2024, the supplementary weighting assigned for a shared
13 operational function in the area of a college and career
14 transition counselor or coordinator shall not count toward this
15 the maximum amount established pursuant to this subparagraph.
16 Criteria for determining the qualification of operational
17 functions for supplementary weighting shall be determined by
18 the department by rule, through consideration of increased
19 student opportunities.
20 (2) Notwithstanding subparagraph (1), for budget years
21 beginning on or after July 1, 2025, if a school district shares
22 an operational function in the area of a school resource
23 officer, then the maximum amount of additional weighting for
24 which the school district shall be eligible in a budget year is
25 twenty-three additional pupils.>
26 2. Page 2, by striking lines 19 and 20 and inserting:
27 <Sec. ___. APPLICABILITY. The following applies July 1,
28 2025, for school budget years beginning on or after that date:
29 The section of this Act amending section 257.11, subsection
30 5, paragraph “a”, subparagraph (1).>
31 3. By renumbering as necessary.
