Sec. 4. JUDICIAL OFFICER ââ UNPAID LEAVE. Notwithstanding the annual salary rates for judicial officers established by this division of this Act for the fiscal year beginning July 1, 2025, and ending June 30, 2026, the supreme court may by order place all judicial officers on unpaid leave status on any day employees of the judicial branch are placed on temporary layoff status.
