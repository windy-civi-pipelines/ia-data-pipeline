6 Section 1. GOALS AND ACCOUNTABILITY —— ECONOMIC
7 DEVELOPMENT.
8 1. For the fiscal year beginning July 1, 2025, the goals
9 for the economic development authority shall be to expand
10 and stimulate the state economy, increase the wealth of
11 Iowans, increase the population of the state, and cultivate an
12 inclusive and welcoming environment for all citizens of Iowa.
13 2. To achieve the goals in subsection 1, the economic
14 development authority shall do all of the following for the
15 fiscal year beginning July 1, 2025:
16 a. Concentrate its efforts on programs and activities that
17 result in commercially viable products and services.
18 b. Adopt practices and services consistent with free
19 market, private sector philosophies.
20 c. Ensure economic growth and development throughout the
21 state.
22 d. Work with businesses and communities to continually
23 improve the economic development climate along with the
24 economic well-being and quality of life for Iowans.
25 e. Coordinate with other state agencies to ensure that they
26 are attentive to the needs of an entrepreneurial culture.
27 f. Establish a strong and aggressive marketing image to
28 showcase Iowa’s workforce, existing industry, and potential.
29 A priority shall be placed on recruiting new businesses,
30 business expansion, and retaining existing Iowa businesses.
31 Emphasis shall be placed on entrepreneurial development through
32 helping entrepreneurs secure capital, and developing networks
33 and a business climate conducive to entrepreneurs and small
34 businesses.
35 g. Encourage the development of communities and quality of
HF 1040.1840 (6) 91
-1- nls/ko 1/18
1 life to foster economic growth.
2 h. Prepare communities for future growth and development
3 through development, expansion, and modernization of
4 infrastructure.
5 i. Develop public-private partnerships with Iowa businesses
6 in the tourism industry, Iowa tour groups, Iowa tourism
7 organizations, and political subdivisions in this state to
8 assist in the development of advertising efforts.
9 j. Collaborate with Iowa’s businesses and communities
10 to evaluate laws adopted by the Iowa legislature to identify
11 negative impacts to the state’s economic development, quality
12 of life, and population growth, with an emphasis on state
13 laws that have made Iowa’s workforce retention, new business
14 recruitment, and population expansion difficult.
