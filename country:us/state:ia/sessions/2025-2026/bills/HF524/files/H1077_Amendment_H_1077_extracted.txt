1 Amend House File 524 as follows:
3 inserting:
4 <Section 1. NEW SECTION. 136D.5 Minors’ use of tanning
5 devices —— restrictions.
6 1. A tanning facility shall not allow a person under
7 eighteen years of age to use a tanning device unless the parent
8 or guardian of the person first provides written consent in
9 person to the tanning facility for the person to use a tanning
10 device, which must include the following statement:
11 UV tanning devices increase the risk of melanoma, a
12 particularly deadly type of skin cancer. UV radiation is most
13 dangerous when exposed at a young age.
14 2. A written consent provided pursuant to subsection 1 shall
15 remain valid for subsequent visits to the tanning facility.
16 However, the written consent may be revoked at any time by the
17 parent or guardian who provided it.>
