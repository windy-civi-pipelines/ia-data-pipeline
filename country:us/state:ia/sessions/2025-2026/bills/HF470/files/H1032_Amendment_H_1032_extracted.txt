1 Amend House File 470 as follows:
2 1. Page 2, after line 31 by inserting:
4 the following new subsection:
5 NEW SUBSECTION. 5. For purposes of this section,
6 “premises”, in addition to premises as defined in section 123.3,
7 may include any of the following noncontiguous locations,
8 provided that such noncontiguous locations are approved by the
9 alcohol and tobacco tax and trade bureau of the United States
10 department of the treasury:
11 a. Any number of locations which are only separated from
12 the premises as defined in section 123.3 by public waterways,
13 roads, or carrier rights-of-way.
14 b. Any number of locations in the same general location as
15 the premises as defined in section 123.3.
16 c. An additional warehouse or warehouses, located somewhere
17 other than on the premises as defined in section 123.3 or a
18 location described in paragraph “a” or “b”.>
19 2. By renumbering as necessary.
