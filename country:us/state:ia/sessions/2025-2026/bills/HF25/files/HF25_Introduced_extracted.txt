NEW SECTION . 701.14 Wearing of mask, clothing, 1 or device that conceals identity of wearer. 2 1. A person who is convicted of a criminal offense who 3 intentionally concealed or attempted to conceal the personâs 4 identity by wearing a mask, or other clothing or device, for 5 the purpose of facilitating the commission of the crime shall 6 have the underlying criminal offense classified and punished as 7 an offense one degree higher than the underlying offense. 8 2. This section shall not apply to any of the following: 9 a. A person wearing a traditional holiday costume in season. 10 b. A person engaged in a trade or occupation for which a 11 mask is worn for the purpose of ensuring the physical safety 12 of the wearer, or because of the nature of the trade or 13 occupation. 14 c. A person using a mask in a theatrical production. 15 d. A person wearing a gas mask prescribed in civil defense 16 drills and exercises or emergencies. 17 e. A person who is a member or member-elect of a society, 18 order, or organization while engaged in any parade, ritual, 19 initiation, ceremony, celebration, or requirement of such 20 society, order, or organization and wearing or using any manner 21 of costume, paraphernalia, disguise, facial makeup, hood, 22 implement, or device, whether the identity of such person is 23 concealed or not, on any public or private street, road, way, 24 or property, or in any public or private building, provided 25 permission shall have been first obtained by a representative 26 of such society, order, or organization from the governing body 27 of the municipality, as defined in section 403.17, in which the 28 same takes place. 29 f. A person wearing a medical or surgical grade mask for 30 the purpose of preventing the spread of contagious disease or 31 infection. 32 g.
