A general contractor, as defined in section 572.1, shall 2 obtain a performance bond payable to the person for whom the 3 general contractor is performing work in the amount of fifty 4 thousand dollars prior to performing any work in this state. 5 The performance bond shall be executed by a surety company 6 authorized to do business in this state. 7 2. The bill does not apply to a general contractor 23 performing work for the state or a political subdivision of 24 the state, including a general contractor subject to state 25 agency competitive bidding requirements.
