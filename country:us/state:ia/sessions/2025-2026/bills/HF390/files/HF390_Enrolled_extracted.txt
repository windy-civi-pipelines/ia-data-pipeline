House File 390 - Enrolled House File 390 AN ACT RELATING TO PHYSICAL EXAMINATIONS OF PERSONNEL WORKING IN LICENSED OR REGISTERED CHILD CARE FACILITIES. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF IOWA: Section 1. Section 237A.5, subsection 1, Code 2025, is amended to read as follows: 1. a. All personnel in licensed or registered child care facilities shall have good health as evidenced by a report following a preemployment physical examination taken . b.
