4 2025, is amended to read as follows:
5 d. New or used motor vehicles designed to carry not more
6 than ten persons as passengers, including the driver, or used
7 passenger vans designed to carry not more than twelve fifteen
8 persons as passengers, including the driver, either school
9 owned or privately owned, which are used to transport pupils
10 to activity events in which the pupils are participants or
11 used to transport pupils to their homes in case of illness or
12 other emergency situations. The vehicles operated under the
13 provisions of this paragraph shall be operated by employees of
14 the school district who are specifically approved by the local
15 superintendent of schools for the assignment.
16 Sec. ___. Section 321.373, subsection 3, paragraph c, Code
17 2025, is amended to read as follows:
18 c. The rules shall allow used passenger vans designed to
19 carry twelve fifteen or fewer passengers, including the driver,
20 and weighing ten thousand pounds or less, to be used as school
21 buses if the van does not carry more passengers than there are
22 safety belts or safety harnesses in the van as installed by
23 the manufacturer. The operator of the van shall comply with
24 the qualification, licensing, and instruction requirements set
25 forth in sections 321.375 and 321.376.>
26 2. Title page, line 1, before <approved> by inserting
27 <the transportation of students, including the use of certain
28 passenger vans as a school bus and>
29 3. By renumbering as necessary.
