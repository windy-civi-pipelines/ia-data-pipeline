Senate File 153 - Enrolled Senate File 153 AN ACT RELATING TO DRIVER AGE QUALIFICATIONS FOR CERTAIN COMMERCIAL MOTOR VEHICLE DRIVERS OPERATING SOLELY INTRASTATE TO TRANSPORT HAZARDOUS MATERIAL. Rules adopted under this section concerning driver age qualifications do not apply to drivers for private and for-hire motor carriers which operate solely intrastate except when the vehicle being driven is transporting a hazardous material in a quantity which requires placarding .
