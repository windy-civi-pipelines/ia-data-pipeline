H.F. 607 This bill amends provisions relating to income eligibility 1 levels for pregnant women and infants under the Medicaid 2 program. 3 Current law provides that an infant or a pregnant woman 4 whose family income is not more than 215 percent of the federal 5 poverty level, if the infant or woman is otherwise eligible, is 6 eligible for Medicaid. The bill amends the income eligibility 7 level to 375 percent. 8 The bill requires the department of health and human 9 services (HHS) to submit a Medicaid state plan amendment to the 10 centers for Medicare and Medicaid services of the United States 11 department of health and human services (CMS) for approval in 12 accordance with the provisions in federal law to provide 12 13 months of continuous postpartum eligibility under the Medicaid 14 program to a pregnant woman whose family income while pregnant 15 is at or below 375 percent of the federal poverty level for 16 the household size, beginning January 1, 2026.
