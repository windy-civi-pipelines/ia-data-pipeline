The commission shall annually limit to six thousand the 3 number of nonresidents allowed to have antlered or any sex deer 4 hunting licenses. Of the six thousand nonresident antlered or 5 any sex deer hunting licenses issued, not less than six hundred 6 of the licenses shall be reserved for nonresident landowners 7 with at least forty acres of land zoned for agricultural 8 purposes in this state. If fewer than six hundred nonresidents 9 apply for a reserved license by the application deadline set by 10 the commission by rule, any license not issued shall be made 11 available for purchase at the same time and in the same manner 12 as other excess nonresident antlered or any sex deer hunting 13 licenses. Not more than thirty-five percent of the issued 14 reserved licenses shall be bow season licenses. Of the six 15 thousand remaining nonresident antlered or any sex deer hunting 16 licenses issued, not more than thirty-five percent of the 17 licenses shall be bow season licenses. After the six thousand 18 antlered or any sex nonresident deer hunting licenses have been 19 issued, all additional licenses shall be issued for antlerless 20 deer only. The commission shall annually determine the number 21 of nonresident antlerless deer only deer hunting licenses that 22 will be available for issuance. 23 Sec. 2. EFFECTIVE DATE.
