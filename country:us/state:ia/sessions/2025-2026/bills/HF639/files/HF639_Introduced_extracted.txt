NEW SECTION . 479B.13A Insurance requirements 1 for permittee. 2 1. In addition to the requirements of section 479B.13, 3 before a permit is granted under this chapter, the applicant 4 shall provide to the commission evidence of an appropriate 5 surety or insurance policy to ensure the payment of all damages 6 resulting from the construction and operation of the hazardous 7 liquid pipeline. The surety or insurance policy must be 8 sufficient to indemnify fully all of the following: 9 a. Any loss arising from or related to any negligent or 10 intentional discharge of content from the pipeline. 11 b. The inability for a person to obtain insurance, or the 15 increased costs for a person to obtain or renew insurance, due 16 to the presence or construction of the pipeline. 17 2.
