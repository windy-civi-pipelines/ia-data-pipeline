The bill 27 requires the department of inspections, appeals, and licensing 28 to inspect an establishment participating in the program 29 as often as it deems necessary to ensure compliance with 30 these requirements. The bill also requires an establishment 31 participating in the program to inform a consumer receiving 32 services from an unlicensed person in writing prior to 33 the provision of services.
