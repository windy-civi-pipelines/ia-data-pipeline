4 <Section 1. Section 483A.24, subsection 16, Code 2025, is
5 amended to read as follows:
6 16. a. Upon payment of the fee established by rules adopted
7 pursuant to section 483A.1 for a lifetime fishing license, or
8 lifetime hunting and fishing combined license, or lifetime
9 fur harvester license, the department shall issue a lifetime
10 fishing license, or lifetime hunting and fishing combined
11 license, or lifetime fur harvester license to a resident of
12 Iowa who has served in the armed forces of the United States on
13 federal active duty and who was disabled or was a prisoner of
14 war during that veteran’s military service.
15 b. Upon payment of a fee established by rules adopted
16 pursuant to section 483A.1 for a nonresident lifetime fishing
17 license, the department shall issue a nonresident lifetime
18 fishing license to a nonresident who has served in the armed
19 forces of the United States on federal active duty and was
20 disabled during that veteran’s military service.
21 c. The department shall prepare an application to be used
22 by a person requesting a lifetime fishing license or lifetime
23 hunting and fishing combined license under this subsection.
24 The department of veterans affairs shall assist the department
25 in verifying the status or claims of applicants under this
26 subsection. As used in this subsection, “disabled” means
27 entitled to a service connected rating under 38 U.S.C. ch. 11.>
28 2. Title page, line 1, by striking <fur harvesting
29 privileges of> and inserting <lifetime licenses issued by the
30 department of natural resources for>
31 3. Title page, line 2, after <veterans> by inserting <,
32 including fur harvester licenses and nonresident fishing
33 licenses>
