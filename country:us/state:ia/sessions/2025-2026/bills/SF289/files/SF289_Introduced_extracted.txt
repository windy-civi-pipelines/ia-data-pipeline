Section 709.21, subsection 1, unnumbered 1 paragraph 1, Code 2025, is amended to read as follows: 2 A person who knowingly views, photographs, or films 3 another person, for the purpose of arousing or gratifying the 4 sexual desire of any person, or who knowingly possesses such 5 photograph or film, commits invasion of privacy if all of the 6 following apply: 7 EXPLANATION 8 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with 9 the explanationâs substance by the members of the general assembly. 10 Under current law, to be guilty of invasion of privacy, a 11 person must knowingly view, photograph, or film another person 12 for the purpose of arousing or gratifying the sexual desire of 13 any person, if the other person does not consent or is unable 14 to consent to being viewed, photographed, or filmed; the other 15 person is in a state of full or partial nudity; and the other 16 person has a reasonable expectation of privacy while in a state 17 of full or partial nudity.
