6 <b. This subsection Paragraph “a” does not apply to a any
7 of the following:
8 (1) A person who operates a motor vehicle owned or leased
9 by a federal, state, or local law enforcement agency if the
10 operation is part of the person’s official duties.
11 (2) A person who operates a motor vehicle in compliance with
12 this subparagraph while possessing, or transporting a passenger
13 who possesses, an affidavit signed by a physician licensed
14 under chapter 148, a chiropractor licensed under chapter 151,
15 or an optometrist licensed under chapter 154 stating that the
16 person or passenger has a medical condition involving extreme
17 sensitivity to light that makes it necessary for a motor
18 vehicle to be equipped with a front windshield, side window, or
19 sidewing that does not meet the minimum measurable standard of
20 transparency established in paragraph “a”.
21 (a) The person or passenger shall carry a copy of the
22 affidavit in the motor vehicle at all times.
23 (b) The person shall not operate on the highway a motor
24 vehicle equipped with a front windshield, a side window to the
25 immediate right or left of the driver, or a sidewing forward
26 of and to the left or right of the driver which does not meet a
27 minimum standard of transparency of thirty-five percent light
28 transmittance.>
29 3. By renumbering as necessary.
