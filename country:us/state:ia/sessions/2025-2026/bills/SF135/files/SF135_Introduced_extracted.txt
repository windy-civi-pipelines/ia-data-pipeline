The department shall issue one validation sticker for 8 each set of registration plates. The sticker shall must 9 specify the month and year of expiration of the registration 10 plates. The sticker shall be displayed only on the rear 11 registration plate, except that the sticker shall be displayed 12 on the front registration plate of a truck tractor. The validation 25 sticker indicates a vehicle is properly registered for the 26 registration year described on the sticker when it is displayed 27 on the vehicleâs registration plate. A person is authorized to 28 apply to renew the registration of a vehicle one month prior to 29 the expiration of the current registration year, and must apply 30 for renewal no later than the last day of the month following 31 the month of registration to avoid a penalty.
