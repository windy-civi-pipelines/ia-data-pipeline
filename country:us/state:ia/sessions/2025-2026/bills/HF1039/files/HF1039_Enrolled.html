Section 602.11101, subsection 1, paragraph e, subparagraph (2), Code 2025, is amended to read as follows: (2) (a) Until July 1, 1986, the county shall remain responsible for the compensation of and operating costs for court employees not presently designated for state financing and for miscellaneous costs of the judicial branch related to furnishings, supplies, and equipment purchased, leased, or maintained for the use of judicial officers, referees, and their staff.
