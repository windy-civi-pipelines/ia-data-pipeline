S.F. 102 The bill requires each board of directors of a school 1 district by July 1, 2026, to require annual, evidence-based 2 training on educator mental wellness and self-identification 3 and mitigation of negative mental health symptoms for 4 all school personnel who hold a license, certificate, 5 authorization, or statement of recognition issued by the board 6 of educational examiners and who have regular contact with 7 students in kindergarten through grade 12. The bill makes 8 a similar requirement for training on student social and 9 emotional wellness and the identification and mitigation of 10 negative mental health symptoms in students. The content of 11 each type of training must be based on nationally recognized 12 best practices. The bill directs the state board of education 13 to adopt rules to determine the qualifications required of a 14 person who conducts each type of training. 15 The bill requires training on educator mental wellness to 16 take place within the first 60 days of the beginning of a 17 school calendar, and training on student social and emotional 18 wellness to take place in January or February. 19 -2- LSB 2200XS (1) 91 jda/jh 2/ 2
