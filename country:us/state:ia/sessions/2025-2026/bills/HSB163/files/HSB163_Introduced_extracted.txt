A person with a valid dock site permit and hoist or slip 3 assignment may construct an enclosed structure to protect a 4 hoist or slip if all of the following apply: 5 a. The enclosed structure is constructed with rust-resistant 6 materials. 7 b. The enclosed structure is no more than thirty feet wide. 8 c.
