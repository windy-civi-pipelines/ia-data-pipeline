Names, addresses, and all 8 personally identifiable information of prize winners, if the 9 prize awarded is one million dollars or more, and the prize 10 winner who elects to remain anonymous specifies that choice at 11 the time of claiming the lottery prize. For purposes of this subsection, the amount of a lottery 20 prize is determined by the total amount of prize money paid 21 to a prize winner for a single lottery prize claim before 22 deducting any federal tax withholdings or other deductions 23 required by law. 24 c.
