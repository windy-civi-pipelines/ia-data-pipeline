Senate File 288 - Enrolled Senate File 288 AN ACT RELATING TO STUDENTS WHO ARE PREGNANT OR WHO RECENTLY GAVE BIRTH WHO ATTEND STATE INSTITUTIONS OF HIGHER EDUCATION GOVERNED BY THE BOARD OF REGENTS AND COMMUNITY COLLEGES. BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF IOWA: Section 1. NEW SECTION . 261K.1 Student parents at institutions. 1. An institution shall allow a student who is pregnant or has recently given birth an additional, reasonable period of time to take examinations that is consistent with the policies of the institution and that is mutually agreed to by the student and the institution. b. An institution shall allow a student who is pregnant or has recently given birth an additional, reasonable period of time to complete a degree or candidacy for a degree that is consistent with the policies of the institution and that is mutually agreed to by the student and the institution. 4. An institution shall allow a student who is pregnant or has recently given birth to take a leave of absence for a reasonable period of time that is consistent with the policies of the institution and that is mutually agreed to by the student and the institution. Following the leave of absence, the student shall be allowed to return to the studentâs program with the same standing as the student left to the extent reasonably possible.
