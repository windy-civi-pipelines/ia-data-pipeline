A simple misdemeanor is 22 punishable by confinement for no more than 30 days and a 23 fine of at least $105 but not more than $855. The bill makes 24 the violation a simple misdemeanor punishable as a scheduled 25 violation resulting in a fine of $260. 26 The bill provides that notwithstanding any other provision 27 of law, the manufacture, delivery, sale, or possession of drug 28 paraphernalia shall not be used to enhance any other controlled 29 substance violation under Code chapter 124. 30 -1- LSB 1783YH (4) 91 as/js 1/ 1
