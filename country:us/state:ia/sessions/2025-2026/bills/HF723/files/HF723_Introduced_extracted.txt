House File 723 - Introduced HOUSE FILE 723 BY LAWLER A BILL FOR An Act requiring a water treatment plant to conduct a test of 1 sewage sludge to detect the presence of a perfluoroalkyl or 2 polyfluoroalkyl substance. 3 BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF IOWA: 4 TLSB 2877YH (2) 91 da/ns H.F. 723 Section 1. NEW SECTION . 455B.211A Perfluoroalkyl or 1 polyfluoroalkyl substances. 2 A waste water treatment plant shall conduct a test of 3 sewage sludge to detect the presence of a perfluoroalkyl or 4 polyfluoroalkyl substance prior to providing the sewage sludge 5 for application on land used for farming. If the test detects 6 the presence of a perfluoroalkyl or polyfluoroalkyl substance, 7 the waste water treatment plant shall deliver a written notice 8 of the test results to the owner of the land where the sewage 9 sludge is to be applied. The owner may refuse to accept the 10 sewage sludge without legal consequence. 11 EXPLANATION 12 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with 13 the explanationâs substance by the members of the general assembly. 14 This bill requires a waste water treatment plant to test 15 sewage sludge for perfluoroalkyl or polyfluoroalkyl substances 16 (PFAS) prior to providing the sewage sludge for application on 17 land used for farming. If the test detects the presence of 18 PFAS, the waste water treatment plant must notify the owner 19 of the land of that fact. The owner may refuse to accept the 20 sewage sludge without legal consequence. 21 -1- LSB 2877YH (2) 91 da/ns 1/ 1
