4 <Section 1. Section 135B.3, Code 2025, is amended to read
5 as follows:
6 135B.3 Licensure.
7 1. No A person or governmental unit, acting severally or
8 jointly with any other person or governmental unit shall not
9 establish, conduct, or maintain a hospital or rural emergency
10 hospital in this state without a license.
11 2. A license shall not be issued or renewed under this
12 chapter for an instrumentality of the government of the
13 People’s Republic of China, an individual who is a citizen
14 of the People’s Republic of China, or a business entity in
15 which a majority interest is owned directly or indirectly by
16 an instrumentality of the government of the People’s Republic
17 of China, or an individual who is a citizen of the People’s
18 Republic of China.
19 3. A license shall not be issued or renewed under this
20 chapter for an instrumentality of the government of the Russian
21 Federation, an individual who is a citizen of the Russian
22 Federation, or a business entity in which a majority interest
23 is owned directly or indirectly by an instrumentality of the
24 government of the Russian Federation, or an individual who is a
25 citizen of the Russian Federation.
26 4. A license shall not be issued or renewed under this
27 chapter for an instrumentality of the government of the
28 Democratic People’s Republic of Korea, an individual who is
29 a citizen of the Democratic People’s Republic of Korea, or a
30 business entity in which a majority interest is owned directly
31 or indirectly by an instrumentality of the government of the
32 Democratic People’s Republic of Korea, or an individual who is
33 a citizen of the Democratic People’s Republic of Korea.
34 Sec. 2. Section 135C.6, subsection 1, Code 2025, is amended
35 to read as follows:
