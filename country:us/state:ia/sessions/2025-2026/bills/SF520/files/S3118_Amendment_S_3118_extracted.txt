11 colleges bureau department, using criteria determined and

13 information system.
14 a. Financial data submitted to the division department by a
15 community college shall be broken down by fund.
16 b. Community colleges shall provide data to the community
17 colleges bureau department by a deadline set by the bureau
18 department. The deadline shall be set for a date that permits
19 the bureau department to include the data in a report submitted
20 for to the state board approval and the general assembly for
21 review by December March 15 of each year by the house and
22 senate standing education committees and the joint subcommittee
23 on education appropriations.

28 to and the general assembly on or before February 1 for review
29 by March 15 of each year. The report must include community
30 college program level educational and employment outcomes
31 reports upon availability of all underlying data.
