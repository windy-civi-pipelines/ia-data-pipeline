The bill allows an instructor employed by a school 10 of barbering and cosmetology arts and sciences to perform 11 barbering and cosmetology arts and sciences services for 12 compensation at the school while not instructing students. A 13 school of barbering and cosmetology arts and sciences where a 14 licensee performs barbering and cosmetology arts and sciences 15 services is not required to obtain a license as a barbering and 16 cosmetology arts and sciences establishment. 17 The bill allows the board of barbering and cosmetology arts 18 and sciences (board) to approve a course of study allowing 19 a student to simultaneously study for licenses to practice 20 both esthetics and nail technology. A student who partially 21 completes a combined course of study for licensure for the 22 practice of esthetics and nail technology is not eligible for 23 licensure for the practice of esthetics or nail technology 24 unless the student has completed the licensure requirements 25 for the practice of esthetics or nail technology. The bill 26 requires the board to adopt rules relating to the combined 27 license for the practice of esthetics and nail technology. 28 The bill prohibits the board from approving a program of 29 study that includes a course of study with substantially lower 30 requirements than the requirements imposed by law or the board 31 for a similar license.
