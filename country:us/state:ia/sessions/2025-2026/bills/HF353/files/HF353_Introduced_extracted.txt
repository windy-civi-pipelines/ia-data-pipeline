Section 43.42, Code 2025, is repealed. 5 EXPLANATION 6 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with 7 the explanationâs substance by the members of the general assembly. 8 This bill prohibits a person from filing nomination papers 9 on behalf of a candidate for a partisan nomination if the 10 candidate has not been a member of the political party for 11 which the candidate seeks nomination for at least one year 12 immediately prior to the date of filing, unless the candidate 13 has not previously cast a ballot in any primary or general 14 election and is not a member of another political party. 15 The bill requires a person to have been registered as 16 a member of a political party for the 30 days immediately 17 preceding a primary election or caucus held by the political 18 party in order to be allowed to vote in that primary election 19 or caucus. This requirement does not apply to a person who has 20 not previously cast a ballot in any primary or general election 21 and who is not a member of another political party. The bill 22 repeals provisions allowing a registered voter to change or 23 declare a political party affiliation on the day of a primary 24 election or by submitting an absentee ballot request form. 25 -3- LSB 2378YH (3) 91 ss/ns 3/ 3
