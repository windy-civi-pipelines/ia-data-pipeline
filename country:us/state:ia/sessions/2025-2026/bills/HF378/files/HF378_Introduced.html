Under current law, 13 the wild animal depredation unit acts as the liaison to the 14 department of natural resources (DNR) for the producers in 15 the state who suffer crop, horticultural product, tree, or 16 nursery damage. The bill changes the language to apply to 17 producers in the state who suffer economic loss to agriculture, 18 horticulture, or silviculture operations. 19 Under current law, DNR is directed to issue depredation 20 permits to a landowner who incurs crop, horticultural product, 21 tree, or nursery damage of $1,000 or more due to wild animals. 22 The bill directs DNR to issue both depredation permits and deer 23 shooting permits to a landowner or a tenant who suffers an 24 economic loss of $1,000 or more to agriculture, horticulture, 25 or silviculture operations due to wild animals. 26 The bill allows conservation officers to issue permits 27 pursuant to the bill. 28 The bill creates a designated deer depredation season from 29 January 11 through January 31.
