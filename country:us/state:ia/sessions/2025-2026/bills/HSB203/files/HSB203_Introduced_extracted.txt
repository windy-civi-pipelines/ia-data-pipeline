Each vehicle operated into or through Iowa in interstate 3 operations using motor fuel, special fuel, or electric fuel 4 acquired in any other state shall carry in or on the vehicle a 5 duplicate or evidence of the permit or license required in this 6 section . A fee not to exceed fifty cents shall be charged The 7 state department of transportation shall adopt rules pursuant 8 to chapter 17A to set the fee for each duplicate or other 9 evidence of a permit or license issued. 10 EXPLANATION 11 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with 12 the explanationâs substance by the members of the general assembly. 13 Under current law, Code section 452A.52 prohibits a motor 14 vehicle, other than a private passenger automobile, from 15 bringing into Iowa any motor fuel or special fuel (including 16 electric fuel stored in batteries) to be used in the operation 17 of the vehicle in Iowa unless that person has paid, or made 18 arrangements in advance with the department of transportation 19 (DOT) for payment of, Iowa fuel taxes on the gallonage consumed 20 while operating the vehicle in Iowa. 21 Additionally, each vehicle operated into or through Iowa 22 in interstate operations using motor fuel, special fuel, or 23 electric fuel acquired in any other state must carry in or on 24 the vehicle a duplicate or evidence of the associated permit 25 or license. The fee for a duplicate or other evidence of such 26 a permit or license is 50 cents. This bill strikes the fee 27 amount and instead requires the DOT to adopt administrative 28 rules to set the fee. 29 Persons choosing not to make advance arrangements with 30 the DOT by procuring a permit or license are not relieved of 31 their responsibility to purchase motor fuel, special fuel, and 32 electric fuel commensurate with their use of Iowaâs highway 33 system.
