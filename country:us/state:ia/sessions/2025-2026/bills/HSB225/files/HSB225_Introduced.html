This bill amends provisions in Code chapter 208 13 regulating the mining of minerals, including surface mining 14 and underground mining, other than for coal, and provides 15 requirements for the reclamation of lands at the mine site. 16 The provisions are administered and enforced by the division 17 of soil and water conservation (division), an administrative 18 unit of the department of agriculture and land stewardship 19 (Code chapter 159). The provisions regulate a person engaged 20 in and controlling the mining operation (operator). This 21 includes a requirement that the operator be issued a license 22 (Code sections 208.7 and 208.8) and register the mine being 23 operated (Code sections 208.9 and 209.15). The registration 24 must be accompanied by a bond or other security. The 25 bonding provisions include requirements for the posting of 26 one bond covering multiple mine sites, and the cancellation 27 or forfeiture of the bond (Code sections 208.23, 208.24, 28 208.25, 208.26, and 208.28). After the completion of mining 29 operations, the operator must engage in the reclamation of the 30 land disturbed by mining operations, which may include grading, 31 stabilizing, and revegetating the disturbed lands (Code section 32 208.17).
