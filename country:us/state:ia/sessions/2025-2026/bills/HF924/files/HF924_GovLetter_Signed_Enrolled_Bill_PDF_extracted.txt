verbal contact at all times with the supervised person, and who
is not intoxicated as provided under the conditions set out
in section 321J.2, subsection 1, or under the influence of an
illegal drug.

8. A parent, guardian, or spouse, who is twenty one eighteen

or the ammunition pursuant hereto-, shall be strictly liable to

of the pistol or revolver or ammunition therefor by that minor.
