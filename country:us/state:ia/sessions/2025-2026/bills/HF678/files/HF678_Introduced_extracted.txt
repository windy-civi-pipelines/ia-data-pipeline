A county shall only authorize raises for 18 county employees subject to contracts negotiated under chapter 19 20 when such raises are accounted for in the certified budget. 20 Authorized raises shall not take effect until on or after the 21 first day of the fiscal year for which the budget is certified. 22 EXPLANATION 23 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with 24 the explanationâs substance by the members of the general assembly. 25 This bill requires a county to include moneys dedicated to 26 county employee contracts negotiated under Code chapter 20 in 27 its proposed expenditures for the annual budget.
