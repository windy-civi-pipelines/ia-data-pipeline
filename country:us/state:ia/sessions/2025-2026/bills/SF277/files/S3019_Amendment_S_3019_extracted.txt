Senate File 277
S-3019
1 Amend Senate File 277 as follows:
3 <Sec. ___. Section 299.1, subsection 4, paragraph b, Code
4 2025, is amended to read as follows:
5 b. The policy or rules adopted by the board of directors
6 of a public school district pursuant to paragraph “a” must not
7 apply to any child allow for a child to be absent from school
8 for any reason that the board of directors of the public school
9 district determines is reasonable, which includes an absence
10 from school for any of the following reasons:
11 (1) Who The child has completed the requirements for
12 graduation in a public school district or has obtained a high
13 school equivalency diploma under chapter 259A.
14 (2) Who The child is excused for sufficient reason by any
15 court of record or judge.
16 (3) While The child is attending religious services or
17 receiving religious instructions or participating in other
18 religious observances.
19 (4) Who The child is unable to attend school due to
20 legitimate medical reasons, including due to illness or a
21 medical appointment.
22 (5) Who The child has an individualized education program
23 that affects the child’s attendance.
24 (6) Who The child has a plan under section 504 of the
25 federal Rehabilitation Act, 29 U.S.C. §794, that affects the
26 child’s attendance.
27 (7) The child is experiencing a family emergency, including
28 a family medical incident.
29 (8) The child is attending a wedding or a funeral service,
30 including traveling to and from the wedding or funeral service.
31 (9) The child is a military applicant undergoing military
32 entrance processing.
33 (10) The child is engaged in military service.
34 (11) The child is participating in an educational or other
35 opportunity approved by the board of directors of the public
