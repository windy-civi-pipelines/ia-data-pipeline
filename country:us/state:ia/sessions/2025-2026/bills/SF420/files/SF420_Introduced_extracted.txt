Senate File 420 - Introduced SENATE FILE 420 BY STAED , ZIMMER , PETERSEN , WEINER , and DOTZLER A BILL FOR An Act relating to Native American mascots in schools. 1 BE IT ENACTED BY THE GENERAL ASSEMBLY OF THE STATE OF IOWA: 2 TLSB 2745XS (2) 91 jda/jh S.F. 420 Section 1. NEW SECTION . 280.36 Prohibition of Native 1 American mascots. 2 1. On or after January 1, 2027, a public or nonpublic school 3 shall not have or adopt a name, symbol, or image that depicts 4 or refers to a Native American tribe, individual, custom, or 5 tradition to be used as a mascot, nickname, logo, letterhead, 6 or team name of the school. 7 2. This section shall not apply to a school under the 8 control of a Native American tribe and under the purview of the 9 bureau of Indian education. 10 EXPLANATION 11 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with 12 the explanationâs substance by the members of the general assembly. 13 This bill relates to Native American mascots in schools. 14 The bill prohibits a public or nonpublic school from having or 15 adopting a name, symbol, or image that depicts or refers to a 16 Native American tribe, individual, custom, or tradition to be 17 used as a mascot, nickname, logo, letterhead, or team name of 18 the school on or after January 1, 2027. This prohibition does 19 not apply to a school under the control of a Native American 20 tribe and under the purview of the bureau of Indian education. 21 -1- LSB 2745XS (2) 91 jda/jh 1/ 1
