2 operating under a fluid milk products annual permit, and
3 including applicability provisions.

th/ns
S.F. _____ H.F. _____
1 Section 1. Section 321E.29B, subsection 1, Code 2025, is
2 amended to read as follows:
3 1. Notwithstanding section 321E.8, the department may issue
4 annual permits for the operation of vehicles or combinations
5 of vehicles transporting fluid milk products to or from a milk
6 plant, receiving station, or transfer station, exceeding the
7 weight limitation of section 321.463 but not exceeding a gross
8 weight of ninety-six one hundred thirty thousand pounds, on
9 primary roads and primary road extensions in cities.
10 Sec. 2. APPLICABILITY. This Act applies on and after
11 the effective date of this Act to fluid milk products annual
12 permits issued on or after that date and to such permits issued
13 prior to that date that are in effect on that date.
14 EXPLANATION
15 The inclusion of this explanation does not constitute agreement with
16 the explanation’s substance by the members of the general assembly.
17 Under current law, the department of transportation (DOT)
18 is authorized to issue annual permits for the operation
19 of vehicles or combinations of vehicles transporting fluid
20 milk products to or from a milk plant, receiving station, or
21 transfer station while exceeding the weight limitation of Code
22 section 321.463 but not exceeding 20,000 pounds per axle, and
23 not exceeding a gross weight of 96,000 pounds, on primary roads
24 and primary road extensions in cities. The fee for a fluid
25 milk products annual permit is $400.
26 This bill authorizes the DOT to issue fluid milk products
27 annual permits for vehicles not exceeding a gross weight of
28 130,000 pounds. The bill applies to fluid milk products annual
29 permits issued on or after the effective date of the bill and
30 to such permits issued prior to that date that are in effect on
31 that date. The bill does not amend the 20,000-pounds-per-axle
32 weight limit under Code section 321.463.
